<p><p>A vacuum former is a machine used to shape plastic sheets into specific forms by using heat and vacuum pressure. The process involves heating a thermoplastic sheet until it becomes pliable, then positioning it over a mold and using a vacuum to draw the sheet tightly around the mold.
