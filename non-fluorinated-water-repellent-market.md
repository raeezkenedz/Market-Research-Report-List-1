<p><ul><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Water-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-fluorinated water repellent market is divided into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based repellents utilize organic solvents to dissolve active ingredients, providing strong water resistance and durability on various surfaces. However, they may have higher environmental impact due to volatile organic compounds. On the other hand, water-based repellents are formulated with water as a primary solvent, offering a more eco-friendly alternative. They are generally easier to apply and clean up, making them suitable for a wider range of applications.</p></p>
