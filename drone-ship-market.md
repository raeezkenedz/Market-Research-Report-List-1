<p><p>The drone ship market can be categorized into three main types: semi-autonomous, autonomous, and remote control systems. Semi-autonomous ships require minimal human intervention, allowing onboard operators to oversee operations while the vessel manages basic tasks. Autonomous ships operate independently, relying on advanced algorithms and sensors to navigate and perform missions without human input. Remote control ships are operated manually via a control station, enabling operators to manage the vessel in real-time from a distance. Each type serves various operational needs and applications.</p></p>
