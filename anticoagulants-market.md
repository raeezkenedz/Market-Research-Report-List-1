<p><p>The anticoagulants market includes various types: </p><p>1. **Platelet Aggregation Inhibitors (PAI)** prevent platelets from clumping and forming clots.</p><p>2. **Low-Molecular-Weight Heparin (LMWH)** offers a predictable anticoagulant effect with less monitoring.</p><p>3. **Direct Thrombin Inhibitors (DTI)** specifically inhibit thrombin to prevent clot formation.</p><p>4. **Direct Factor Xa Inhibitors (DFXa)** target Factor Xa to interrupt the coagulation cascade.</p><p>5. **Vitamin K Antagonists (VKA)** block the action of vitamin K, essential for clotting.</p><p>6. **Others** include newer agents and combination therapies, expanding treatment options.</p></p>
