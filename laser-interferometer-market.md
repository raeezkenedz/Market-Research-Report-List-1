<p><strong>The Laser Interferometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homodyne Laser Interferometer</li><li>Heterodyne Laser Interferometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser interferometer market comprises two main types: homodyne and heterodyne interferometers. Homodyne laser interferometers utilize a single frequency laser source to measure phase shifts, providing high precision in displacement measurements ideal for applications like optics testing and surface profiling. In contrast, heterodyne laser interferometers use two distinct frequencies, enabling higher measurement speeds and improved sensitivity, making them suitable for dynamic measurements in fields such as manufacturing and aerospace. Both types serve critical roles in precision engineering and metrology.</p></p>
