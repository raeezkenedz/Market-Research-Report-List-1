<p><p>Hepatitis marker testing involves the detection of specific antibodies and antigens related to different types of hepatitis viruses. The market is segmented into Hepatitis A, B, C, and D. Hepatitis A testing usually focuses on the presence of anti-HAV IgM antibodies, while Hepatitis B testing includes surface antigens (HBsAg) and antibodies (anti-HBs). Hepatitis C primarily involves testing for HCV antibodies and viral RNA, and Hepatitis D testing requires the presence of Hepatitis B due to their interdependence. Each market segment has distinct diagnostic and therapeutic needs.</p></p>
