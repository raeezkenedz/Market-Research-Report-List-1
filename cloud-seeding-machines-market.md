<p><ul><li>Aerial Cloud Seeding Equipment</li><li>Ground-Based Cloud Seeding Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cloud seeding machines market is divided into two primary types: aerial cloud seeding equipment and ground-based cloud seeding equipment. Aerial cloud seeding involves aircraft that disperse seeding agents directly into targeted clouds to enhance precipitation. In contrast, ground-based cloud seeding utilizes stationary or mobile systems that release seeding materials into the atmosphere, typically from the Earth's surface, often relying on natural updrafts to carry the agents into clouds. Both types aim to increase rainfall and manage water resources.</p></p>
