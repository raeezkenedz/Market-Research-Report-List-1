<p><strong>The Current Monitoring Relays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Under-Current Detection</li><li>Over-Current Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Current Monitoring Relays market is primarily segmented into two types: Under-Current Detection and Over-Current Detection. Under-Current Detection relays identify when current levels fall below predefined thresholds, which can indicate potential equipment malfunctions or failures. Conversely, Over-Current Detection relays monitor and trigger alarms when current levels exceed safe limits, preventing overheating and fire risks. Both types are essential for ensuring operational safety, protecting electrical systems, and maintaining optimal performance in various industrial applications.</p></p>
