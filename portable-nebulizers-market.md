<p><strong>The Portable Nebulizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Nebulizers</li><li>Ultrasonic Nebulizers</li><li>Mesh Nebulizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable nebulizers market comprises three primary types: pneumatic, ultrasonic, and mesh nebulizers. Pneumatic nebulizers, also known as jet nebulizers, use compressed air to convert liquid medication into mist. Ultrasonic nebulizers utilize high-frequency sound waves to aerosolize medication, offering a quieter operation and faster treatment. Mesh nebulizers employ a mesh vibratory mechanism to create a fine mist, enhancing portability and efficiency. Each type caters to diverse patient needs, making nebulization accessible and effective for various respiratory conditions.</p></p>
