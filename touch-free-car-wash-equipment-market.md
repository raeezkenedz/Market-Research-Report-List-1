<p><p>The touch-free car wash equipment market is primarily divided into two types: gantry car wash equipment and conveyor tunnel car wash equipment. Gantry car wash systems utilize a stationary framework that moves over the vehicle, applying cleaning solutions and rinsing without physical contact. In contrast, conveyor tunnel car wash systems guide vehicles through a continuous belt-driven tunnel, utilizing various cleaning tools and methods while the vehicle remains stationary. Both systems emphasize efficiency and provide effective cleaning while minimizing risk of damage.</p></p>
