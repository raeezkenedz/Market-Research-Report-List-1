<p><strong>The Fireproofing Coatings for Wood Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fireproofing coatings for wood can be categorized into two main types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based fireproofing coatings utilize water as a primary solvent, offering lower VOC emissions and easier cleanup, making them environmentally friendly. In contrast, solvent-based coatings use organic solvents, providing superior adhesion and durability, ideal for harsher environments. Both types enhance wood's fire resistance, but their selection depends on the specific application, environmental considerations, and desired performance characteristics.</p></p>
