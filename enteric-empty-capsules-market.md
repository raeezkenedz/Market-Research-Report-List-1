<p><p>The enteric empty capsules market is primarily segmented into two types: gelatin and HPMC (hydroxypropyl methylcellulose). Gelatin capsules, derived from animal sources, are widely used for their easy digestibility but may not be suitable for vegetarian or vegan consumers. In contrast, HPMC capsules are plant-based, providing a suitable alternative for those dietary preferences. Both types are designed to protect sensitive ingredients from stomach acid, ensuring targeted release in the intestines, thus enhancing the efficacy of numerous pharmaceutical and dietary formulations.</p></p>
