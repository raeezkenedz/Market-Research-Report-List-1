<p><p>The PVC pipes market is categorized into rigid and flexible types. Rigid PVC pipes are strong, durable, and used in applications requiring high structural integrity, such as water supply, drainage, and irrigation. They resist corrosion and chemicals, making them ideal for long-term use. Flexible PVC pipes, on the other hand, are lightweight, easy to install, and suitable for applications requiring bending, such as underground wiring, landscaping, and low-pressure systems. Both types offer cost-effectiveness and versatility, catering to diverse industrial and residential needs.</p></p>
