<p><strong>The Laminated Labels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solvent-Based</li><li>UV Curable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laminated labels market comprises three main types: water-based, solvent-based, and UV curable. Water-based labels utilize a water-soluble adhesive, offering a more environmentally friendly option with lower emissions. Solvent-based labels, using organic solvents for adhesive, provide strong bonding but may have environmental concerns due to volatile organic compounds. UV curable labels leverage ultraviolet light to cure the adhesive, resulting in rapid drying and durability, making them suitable for high-speed production while also minimizing solvent use.</p></p>
