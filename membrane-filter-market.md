<p><p>The membrane filter market comprises several types, each suited for specific applications. Inorganic membrane filters, often made from ceramics, provide high thermal and chemical resistance. PVDF filters are known for their versatility and compatibility with aggressive solvents. PTFE filters excel in chemical filtration due to their non-stick properties and broad chemical resistance. PES filters are favored for their high flow rates and hydrophilic characteristics. Other types may include nylon, cellulose acetate, and mixed cellulose esters, catering to varied filtration needs across industries.</p></p>
