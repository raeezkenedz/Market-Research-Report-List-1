<p><ul><li>Horizontal Type</li><li>Vertical Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerosol separator market is segmented into horizontal and vertical types, each catering to specific applications. Horizontal aerosol separators utilize a flat design, enabling efficient gas flow and particle separation in industries like pharmaceuticals and food processing. In contrast, vertical aerosol separators orient particles downward, enhancing gravitational settling and often featuring compact designs for space-constrained environments. Both types are crucial in air quality management, industrial processes, and environmental control, effectively capturing harmful aerosols and improving overall air cleanliness.</p></p>
