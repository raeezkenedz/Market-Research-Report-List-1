<p><ul><li>Selective Picking Machine</li><li>Non-Selective Picking Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tea picker market consists of two main types: selective and non-selective picking machines. Selective picking machines are designed to harvest only the tender leaves, allowing for higher quality tea production and less waste. In contrast, non-selective picking machines harvest all leaves indiscriminately, making them more suitable for bulk production. Both types cater to different operational needs and financial capacities, influencing their adoption based on factors such as labor availability, cost-efficiency, and desired tea quality.</p></p>
