<p><p>The dispersing agents market is categorized into **anionic type**, **cationic type**, and **others**. **Anionic dispersing agents** carry a negative charge, making them effective for stabilizing particles in aqueous solutions, commonly used in paints, coatings, and ceramics. **Cationic dispersing agents** have a positive charge, ideal for applications in wastewater treatment and paper manufacturing. The **others** category includes non-ionic and amphoteric types, offering versatility in industries like textiles and agriculture, where charge neutrality or dual functionality is required for particle dispersion.</p></p>
