<p><strong>The Cart Paint Sprayer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HVLP</li><li>Airless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cart paint sprayer market primarily consists of two types: HVLP (High Volume Low Pressure) and airless sprayers. HVLP sprayers utilize a high volume of air at low pressure to atomize paint, ensuring minimal overspray and greater control, making them ideal for detailed work and fine finishes. In contrast, airless sprayers operate by forcing paint through a small tip under high pressure, enabling faster coverage on larger surfaces. Both types cater to different needs in applications ranging from DIY projects to professional painting.</p></p>
