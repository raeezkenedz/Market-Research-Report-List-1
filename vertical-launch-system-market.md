<p><strong>The Vertical Launch System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Launch</li><li>Cold Launch</li><li>Concentric Canister Launch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vertical Launch System (VLS) market comprises three primary launch types: Hot Launch, Cold Launch, and Concentric Canister Launch. Hot Launch involves igniting the missile's engines while still in the launch platform, allowing for rapid deployment. Cold Launch uses gas or other ejecting mechanisms to launch the missile before its engines ignite, minimizing risk to the launcher. Concentric Canister Launch combines features of both, housing multiple missiles in a single canister, optimizing space and improving launch synchronization and readiness.</p></p>
