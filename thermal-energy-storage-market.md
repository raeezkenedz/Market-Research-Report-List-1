<p><strong>The Thermal Energy Storage Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sensible Heat Storage</li><li>Latent Heat Storage</li><li>Thermochemical Storage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal energy storage market encompasses three main types: sensible heat storage, latent heat storage, and thermochemical storage. Sensible heat storage involves storing heat in materials like water or rocks by increasing their temperature. Latent heat storage captures energy during phase changes, such as ice melting, providing efficient energy management. Thermochemical storage utilizes chemical reactions to store and release heat, offering high energy density and long-term storage capabilities. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing energy efficiency and supporting renewable energy integration.</p></p>
