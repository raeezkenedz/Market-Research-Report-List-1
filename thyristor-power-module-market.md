<p><p>The Thyristor Power Module market is categorized into two main types: Unidirectional Thyristors and Bidirectional Thyristors. Unidirectional Thyristors allow current to flow in only one direction, making them suitable for applications like DC power control and rectification. In contrast, Bidirectional Thyristors enable current to flow in both directions, making them ideal for AC applications such as motor control and power regulation. Each type serves specific industrial needs, highlighting their importance in various power electronic applications.</p></p>
