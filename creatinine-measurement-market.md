<p><strong>The Creatinine Measurement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Jaffe's Kinetic Method</li><li>Enzymatic Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The creatinine measurement market primarily features two main types of methods: Jaffe's Kinetic Method and Enzymatic Method. The Jaffe's Kinetic Method is based on the colorimetric reaction of creatinine with picric acid, leading to an absorbance measurement, making it widely used due to its simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, the Enzymatic Method employs specific enzymes to quantify creatinine, offering higher specificity and reduced interference from other substances, thus gaining traction in clinical and diagnostic settings for its accuracy and reliability.</p></p>
