<p><p>The Patch Passive Buzzer market can be categorized into two primary types: Fixed and Pogo Pin. Fixed buzzers are static components mounted onto a substrate, offering durability and ease of installation in electronic devices. In contrast, Pogo Pin buzzers feature spring-loaded contacts that facilitate temporary connections, allowing for flexibility in applications and ease of replacement. Both types serve similar functions in providing audible alerts, but their design and application contexts differ significantly, catering to diverse user needs in electronics.</p></p>
