<p><p>Carousel storage systems are automated storage solutions designed to efficiently organize and retrieve items. There are two main types: horizontal and vertical carousel storage systems. Horizontal carousel systems consist of a series of horizontally rotating shelves that bring items to the operator, ideal for large and wide products. In contrast, vertical carousel systems feature rotating trays arranged vertically, optimizing space for smaller items and maximizing storage density. Both types enhance inventory management and improve retrieval speed in various industries.</p></p>
