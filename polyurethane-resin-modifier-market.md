<p><strong>The Polyurethane Resin Modifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monofunctional Modifier</li><li>Multifunctional Modifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane resin modifier market consists of two main types: monofunctional modifiers and multifunctional modifiers. Monofunctional modifiers enhance specific properties of polyurethane resins, such as improving flexibility or adhesion without significantly altering the resin’s chemical structure. In contrast, multifunctional modifiers provide multiple benefits by targeting various properties, including flexibility, toughness, and thermal stability, all in one formulation. Both types are essential for customizing polyurethane products to meet diverse performance requirements across industries like coatings, adhesives, and construction.</p></p>
