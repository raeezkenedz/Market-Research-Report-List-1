<p><p>The Scratch Testers market comprises three main types: Ordinary Scratch Testers, Linear Scratch Testers, and Multi Finger Scratch Testers. Ordinary Scratch Testers assess material scratch resistance using a single-point approach. Linear Scratch Testers utilize a linear motion to create consistent scratch patterns for more precise measurements. Multi Finger Scratch Testers evaluate the durability of coatings and surfaces by simulating the effects of multiple contact points, offering a comprehensive analysis of material performance under diverse conditions.</p></p>
