<p><p>Vane anemometers are categorized into two main market types: integral and split types. Integral vane anemometers feature a compact design where the measuring probe and display unit are combined, making them portable and user-friendly for quick measurements. In contrast, split-type vane anemometers consist of a separate probe and display unit, allowing for greater flexibility in positioning the probe in inaccessible areas. Both types are used for measuring air velocity in HVAC systems, wind tunnels, and environmental assessments.</p></p>
