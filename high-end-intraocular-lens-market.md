<p><strong>The High-end Intraocular Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spherical Lens</li><li>Aspherical Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-end intraocular lens market features two primary types: spherical and aspherical lenses. Spherical lenses have a consistent curvature, providing reliable vision correction but may produce undesirable optical effects, such as halos at night. In contrast, aspherical lenses are designed with varying curvature, improving image quality and reducing aberrations, thereby enhancing visual acuity, especially in low-light conditions. Aspherical lenses are increasingly preferred for premium cataract surgery due to their advanced optical performance and patient satisfaction.</p></p>
