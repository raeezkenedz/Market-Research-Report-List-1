<p><p>The bone repair materials market includes bone grafts and bone cements, both essential for orthopedic surgeries and tissue regeneration. Bone grafts, derived from natural or synthetic sources, promote new bone growth by providing a scaffold for tissue formation. Bone cements, typically made from polymethyl methacrylate (PMMA), serve as adhesives to stabilize bone structures and fix implants. Both materials are crucial for treating fractures, joint replacements, and spinal surgeries, enhancing patient recovery and restoring functionality.</p></p>
