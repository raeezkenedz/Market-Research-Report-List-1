<p><p>Continuous form paper is categorized into single-layer and multi-layer markets based on its structure and functionality. Single-layer market products consist of a single sheet that allows for printing, often used for invoices or receipts. In contrast, multi-layer market products have multiple layers, enabling the creation of carbonless copies or duplicate documents simultaneously, making them ideal for official forms, shipping documents, and record-keeping. Each type serves distinct purposes in business operations, facilitating efficient documentation and communication.</p></p>
