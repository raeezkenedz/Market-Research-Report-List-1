<p><p>The Electronic Voltage Transformer (EVT) market consists of two main types: Active and Passive Electronic Voltage Transformers. Active EVTs utilize electronic systems to measure and convert high voltages into standardized outputs, offering higher accuracy and flexibility in applications. Conversely, Passive EVTs operate without active components, relying on traditional transformer principles, making them simpler and often more cost-effective for basic voltage measurement tasks. Both types serve essential roles in power system monitoring and protection, catering to different market needs and technical requirements.</p></p>
