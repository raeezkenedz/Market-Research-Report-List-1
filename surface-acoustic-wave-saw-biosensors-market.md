<p><ul><li>Delay-line type</li><li>Resonators Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) biosensor market comprises two primary types: delay-line and resonator types. Delay-line SAW biosensors utilize a long acoustic path, enabling detection of mass changes through time delays in the signal. Resonator SAW biosensors, on the other hand, operate based on frequency shifts caused by binding events, offering high sensitivity in compact designs. Both types are widely used in applications like medical diagnostics, environmental monitoring, and food safety, leveraging their rapid response times and accuracy.</p></p>
