<p><p>The tactile actuator market is primarily segmented into ERM (Eccentric Rotating Mass) actuators, LRAS (Linear Resonant Actuators), and other types. ERM actuators generate tactile feedback through rotating masses, commonly used in smartphones and gaming devices. LRAS actuators provide precise and efficient haptic feedback through linear movement, ideal for wearables and automotive applications. Other types may include piezoelectric and magnetic actuators. This diversity caters to varying user experiences across different devices, enhancing interactivity and user engagement.</p></p>
