<p><p>The PETG plastics market comprises three main types: extruded grade, injection molding grade, and blow molding grade. Extruded grade is primarily used for sheets and films, offering high clarity and flexibility. Injection molding grade is designed for producing intricate parts with excellent detail and surface finish, often used in packaging and consumer products. Blow molding grade is utilized for creating lightweight, hollow containers, such as bottles, providing durability and resistance to impact. Each type serves distinct applications based on processing methods.</p></p>
