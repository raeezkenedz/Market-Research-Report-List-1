<p><p>The Roller Hearth Kiln market is divided into continuous and batch kilns based on operational methods. Continuous kilns operate continuously, allowing for a steady flow of materials through the heating process, making them ideal for high-volume production. In contrast, batch kilns process materials in distinct batches, offering flexibility in production volumes and types but typically at a slower pace. Both types serve various industries, including ceramics and glass, with distinct advantages in efficiency and control over the heating process.</p></p>
