<p><strong>The Marine Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anti-corrosion Coating</li><li>Antifouling Coating</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine coatings market is primarily categorized into three types: anti-corrosion coatings, antifouling coatings, and others. Anti-corrosion coatings protect vessels and structures from rust and damage caused by seawater exposure, ensuring longevity. Antifouling coatings, on the other hand, prevent the growth of marine organisms on hulls, enhancing performance and fuel efficiency. The "others" segment includes specialty coatings such as waterproofing, thermal insulation, and decorative finishes that cater to specific marine industry needs. Together, these coatings enhance durability and efficiency in marine applications.</p></p>
