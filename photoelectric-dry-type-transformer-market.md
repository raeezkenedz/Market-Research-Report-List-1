<p><p>The photoelectric dry type transformer market comprises two main types: casting type and lapping type. Casting type transformers utilize resin impregnation to enhance insulation and heat dissipation, making them suitable for various industrial applications. In contrast, lapping type transformers employ a winding process with overlapping insulation layers, allowing for compact designs and improved efficiency. Both types are preferred for their low maintenance, environmental safety, and reduced fire risks, catering to energy, manufacturing, and infrastructure sectors.</p></p>
