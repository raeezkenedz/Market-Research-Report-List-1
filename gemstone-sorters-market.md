<p><p>Gemstone sorters are categorized into three main types: X-ray, electromagnetic, and others. X-ray sorters utilize high-energy radiation to identify and separate gemstones based on their density and composition, providing high precision. Electromagnetic sorters use magnetic fields to distinguish gemstones based on their magnetic properties, enhancing the separation process. Other types may include optical or manual sorting systems, which offer additional techniques for gemstone classification. Each type serves to improve efficiency and accuracy in the gemstone sorting process.</p></p>
