<p><p>The Variable Frequency Drives (VFD) market encompasses three primary types: AC drivers, DC drivers, and servo drivers. AC drivers regulate the speed and torque of AC motors by varying frequency and voltage, making them ideal for various industrial applications. DC drivers control the speed of DC motors, providing precise speed management for applications requiring higher torque at lower speeds. Servo drivers enhance motion control in servo motors, ensuring accuracy and responsiveness in automated systems, particularly in robotics and CNC machinery.</p></p>
