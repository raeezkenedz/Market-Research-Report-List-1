<p><strong>The Aluminium Pigments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Leafing Aluminium Pigments</li><li>Non-Leafing Aluminium Pigments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum pigments market is primarily categorized into two types: leafing and non-leafing aluminum pigments. Leafing aluminum pigments have a distinctive property of floating to the surface when mixed in a medium, creating a shiny, reflective finish. They are commonly used in coatings, inks, and plastics. In contrast, non-leafing aluminum pigments remain dispersed throughout the medium, providing a more subdued metallic appearance. This type is often utilized in applications requiring uniform color and opacity, such as automotive paints and industrial coatings.</p></p>
