<p><p>The thrust ball bearing market is categorized into two main types: single-direction bearings and double-direction bearings. Single-direction bearings support axial loads in one direction, making them suitable for applications where the load moves in a single direction. In contrast, double-direction bearings accommodate axial loads in both directions, allowing for greater versatility in applications where opposing forces are present. These bearings are essential in machinery and equipment where precise rotational movement and load handling are crucial for performance.</p></p>
