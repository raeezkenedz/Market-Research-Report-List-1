<p><p>Inflammatory heart diseases encompass various conditions affecting the heart's structure and function, significantly impacting the market for treatments. Atherosclerosis involves the buildup of plaques in arteries, leading to cardiovascular complications. Myocarditis refers to the inflammation of the heart muscle, often resulting from infections or autoimmune disorders. Pericarditis is the inflammation of the outer lining of the heart, causing chest pain and potential cardiac complications. Each type presents unique challenges and treatment opportunities, driving the development of targeted therapies and healthcare solutions.</p></p>
