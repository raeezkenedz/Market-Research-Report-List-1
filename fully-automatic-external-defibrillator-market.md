<p><p>The Fully Automatic External Defibrillator (AED) market comprises two primary types: non-synchronous and synchronous defibrillators. Non-synchronous defibrillators deliver shocks without the need for detection of heart rhythms, making them user-friendly and effective during sudden cardiac events. Synchronous defibrillators, on the other hand, synchronize shocks with the cardiac cycle, which is crucial for treating specific arrhythmias. Both types play vital roles in emergency medical response, enhancing the chances of survival during cardiac emergencies in various settings.</p></p>
