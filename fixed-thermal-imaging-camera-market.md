<p><strong>The Fixed Thermal Imaging Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Near Infrared</li><li>Mid Infrared</li><li>Far Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fixed thermal imaging camera market is categorized into three types based on wavelength range: near infrared, mid-infrared, and far infrared. Near-infrared cameras capture thermal radiation primarily from 0.7 to 1.5 micrometers, making them ideal for low-light conditions. Mid-infrared cameras, covering 3 to 5 micrometers, detect heat emissions from objects, suitable for industrial and surveillance applications. Far infrared cameras operate beyond 8 micrometers, focusing on heat detection from living beings and machinery, widely used in search-and-rescue and fire detection.</p></p>
