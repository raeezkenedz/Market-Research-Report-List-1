<p><ul><li>Thermoset</li><li>Thermo-plastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UD Glass Fiber Prepreg market consists primarily of two types: thermoset and thermoplastic. Thermoset prepregs, which cure by chemical cross-linking, offer enhanced rigidity and thermal resistance, making them ideal for structural applications in aerospace and automotive industries. In contrast, thermoplastic prepregs can be reprocessed and reshaped upon heating, providing flexibility and toughness, which is advantageous for applications requiring durability and impact resistance. Both types cater to diverse industry needs, balancing performance and manufacturing efficiency.</p></p>
