<p><p>The refractory mortar market can be categorized into several types based on the primary materials used. Clay refractory mortar is known for its thermal stability and moderate resistance to chemical attacks, making it suitable for general applications. High alumina refractory mortar offers superior strength and thermal resistance, ideal for high-temperature applications. Silicon refractory mortar provides excellent resistance to thermal shock and oxidizing environments. Lastly, magnesia refractory mortar is characterized by its durability and resistance to basic slag attack, commonly used in steelmaking and industrial furnaces.</p></p>
